Our Story

Modern Taste with Kutaisi Character

MEDEA Bar-Restaurant is located in central Kutaisi on St. Nino 17. Our concept combines Georgian warmth with a refined, contemporary dining style.

We focus on quality ingredients, polished service, and a comfortable premium atmosphere throughout the day.
